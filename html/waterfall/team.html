TEAM

This project was created in 2002 by a small team of people.

Richard Lord produced the project, choreographing and directing the dance and designing and programming the game.

Kate Health composed the music.

Emma Diamond is the dancer.

Julian Blom was an advisor for the project

Greville Matthews was the production assistant.

The project was funded by the Arts Council of England. We received assistance from The Place Choreodrome and subsidised rehearsal facilities from Diamond Studio.

Richard Lord - Game Developer, Choreographer - www.richardlord.net
