Sponsors

The Combined Arts department of the Arts Council of England provided us with seed funding to develop the project through the early planning stages.

Virtual Presence, a specialist hardware supplier based in London, lent us the motion capture hardware for the early development of the project during Digital Dancing 1997 amd 1998 and Fonster Europe 1998.

Kaydara, a software development company based in Canada, lent us a copy of their Filmbox software to use during the research for this project. This is the software we use for the live motion capture during performances.
